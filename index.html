Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is made with a hearty meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella. It's the ultimate comfort food for any family dinner.

While it takes some time to assemble, the layers of flavor make every minute worth the effort!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb Ground beef
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Marinara sauce
  • Fresh parsley
  • Recipe Title 1
  • Recipe Title 2
  • Recipe Title 3

Steps

  1. Boil the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
  2. Brown the ground beef in a large skillet and add marinara sauce.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix the ricotta cheese with fresh parsley.
  4. Layer the noodles, meat sauce, and cheese mixture in a baking dish.
  5. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30 minutes or until bubbly and golden.
